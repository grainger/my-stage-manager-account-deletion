My Stage Manager – Account & data deletion

This page explains how users can request deletion of their My Stage Manager account and associated data.

In-app deletion (recommended)

  1. Open the My Stage Manager app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion request.

Alternative (email) request

If you cannot access your account, email us at kl1mg.gb@gmail.com and include the email address associated with your account.

What happens when your account is deleted

  • We delete account profile information and login credentials.
  • We delete event/stage management data stored with your account.
  • Some information may be retained for up to 30 days before permanent deletion to comply with legal, fraud-prevention, or debugging requirements.

Deletion requests are processed within 7 days (usually sooner).
